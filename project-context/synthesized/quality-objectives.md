## Project Goal

- Oracle wants to improve the productivity and visibility of its software development team's activities by 20%.
- The proposed product direction is an administration portal plus a Telegram chatbot that automates task creation and gives visibility into completed work.

## Success Metrics

### Task Creation Efficiency

- Average task creation time, comparing manual flow versus AI-assisted flow.
- Percentage of tasks created with AI.
- Administrative time saved.

### Sprint Output And Task Quality

- Story points obtained during the sprint.
- Percentage of tasks with complete fields.
- Percentage of tasks accepted without changes.
- Percentage of tasks corrected manually.
- Percentage of tasks completed during the sprint.

### Project Visibility And Classification

- Percentage of tasks with project and priority defined.
- Quality of classification from the moment of creation.
